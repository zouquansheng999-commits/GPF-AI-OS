# Code of Conduct

GPF-AI-OS is committed to building an open, welcoming, and respectful community.

## Our Standards

We encourage:

- Respectful discussions
- Constructive feedback
- Inclusive collaboration
- Honest research
- Transparent communication

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment
- Personal attacks
- Hate speech
- Spam
- Intentional misinformation

## Enforcement

Project maintainers reserve the right to remove contributions that violate these principles.

Thank you for helping make GPF-AI-OS a positive community.
